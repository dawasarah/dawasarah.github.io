A budget is a quantitative and qualitative statement which shows both short term and long term plans which are to be implemented by an organisation to achieve set objectives.

Benefits of budget

  1. Provides variable means of controlling income and expenditure.
  2. Provides a tool by which managerial policies are periodically evaluated.
  3. Helps in capital and other resource allocation.
  4. Provides a yardstick for measuring the performance of the department and individuals working in the department.

Classification of budgets

According to time. According to function. According to flexibility
